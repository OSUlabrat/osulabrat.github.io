Eye Spy Game

Technology and Programming Languages: Agile Build, Android Studio, Java, XML, libGDX

Catch the Criminal is a eye spy game that challenged the user to find who was the criminal in a sea of individuals. It supports multiple levels of varying difficulty degrees, up to 30 concurrent sprites on canvas with collision detection, and a reward system.
